What is our purpose?

  • Bring like-minded people together
  • Build experimental intelligence architectures
  • Learn by implementing neural networks
  • Discuss the state of AI and the nature of intelligence

What are we currently working on?

As the 2022 Spring semester comes to a close, we are recruiting to hit the ground running come Fall 2022.

Positions of leadership are available and ultimately the specific direction the club decides to move in next year will be decided by the members, which could include you!

Here are some potential ideas:

  • The first piece of learning hardware since the original perceptron through FPGAs that reprogram themselves
  • A proof of concept for a neural network without an omnipitent learning function, like gradient descent
  • A custom deep learning framework in Haskell
  • An end-to-end trained robot

How do I join?

To get in the loop, join our Discord Server. Outside of meetings, this is where club ongoings are detailed.

Feel free to come check out a meeting even if you are not yet a member! (Places and times will be listed here once decided)

You may also contact hello@pomona.computer.
